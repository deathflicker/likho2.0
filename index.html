What is this all about?

'Likho' is a new way of memorizing things. It works as a digital notepad. You can write your important notes, use it as a ToDo list, jot down the birth date of your loved ones, and more! We respect your privacy. Hence, your data is completely backed up locally on your device.

New Updates (v 0.2.0)

  • Now you can write a note and save it for future!
  • Functionality to add multi-line notes.
  • "Clear All" button helps to clear all the notes at once.
  • "Scroll to top" button added.
  • Minor changes to the UI and it's elements.
  • Seamlessly contact the developer on WhatsApp.
  • More polished experience.

How to get started?

  • First and foremost, write something in the provided textbox.

  • Select a color you would like to apply to your text.

  • Press the 'Add' button to make a new entry on your list.

  • You can scroll through the items you have just noted.

What are the pros?

  • User friendly.

  • Works on almost every possible mobile device.

  • Store your notes for future use.

  • Extremely lightweight application.

  • Seamless performance.

  • Choose colors as per the importance of your note.

What are the cons?

Does not provide cross-device syncing yet.
